Event Schedule

A full-day program from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on October 18th, 2025.

  • 10:00 AM – 10:30 AM
    Arrival & Registration
    Guest check-in and badge collection. Welcome packs distributed.
  • 10:30 AM – 10:45 AM
    Opening Ceremony
    Welcome speech by the TEDxAsia Pacific U President and an overview of the day's agenda.
  • 10:45 AM – 12:00 PM
    Session 1
    Featuring Speaker 1, Speaker 2, and Speaker 3, followed by an interactive segment.
  • 12:00 PM – 12:30 PM
    Tea Break
    Light refreshments provided. Attendees can mingle and explore the interactive space.
  • 12:30 PM – 1:45 PM
    Session 2
    Featuring Speaker 4, Speaker 5, and Speaker 6, with a brief TEDx video clip.
  • 1:45 PM – 2:45 PM
    Lunch & Exploration Hour
    Catered lunch. Attendees can interact with sponsor booths and student-led exhibits.
  • 2:45 PM – 4:15 PM
    Session 3
    Featuring the final set of speakers (7-10) and a session wrap-up.
  • 4:15 PM – 4:45 PM
    Closing Ceremony
    Thank you note from the organizing team and acknowledgement of partners.
  • 4:30 PM – 5:00 PM
    Farewell & Networking
    Open networking session with speakers and attendees.
