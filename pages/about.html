About TEDxAsiaPacificU

Ideas Worth Spreading, born right here at APU.

Our Mission

TEDxAsia Pacific U is a university-level TEDx event officially licensed by TED, set to take place on 11th October 2025 at Asia Pacific University (APU). This event marks the first time APU will host a TEDx event of this scale, with the goal of inspiring dialogue, igniting curiosity, and building a community centered around meaningful ideas. The theme of our inaugural event is “Beyond the Surface”. We aim to create a space for powerful storytelling, knowledge sharing, and community-driven innovation.

Our Goals

  • Build a platform for sharing transformative ideas from students, professionals, and visionaries across disciplines.
  • Foster a culture of curiosity, dialogue, and critical thinking by creating an environment where ideas are freely exchanged.
  • Empower students through real-world event management and gain hands-on experience in logistics, marketing, and leadership.
  • Showcase local innovation, sustainability efforts, and cultural expression that align with themes of progress.
  • Establish TEDxAsia Pacific U as an annual flagship event at APU, building a legacy of high-impact storytelling.
